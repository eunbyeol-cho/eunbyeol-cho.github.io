Eunbyeol Cho

I am a Ph.D. student at KAIST Graduate School of AI, advised by Professor Edward Choi. My research interests lie in Healthcare AI and Natural Language Processing. I am particularly interested in text-based EHR synthesis, including its real-world applications, evaluation, and the hallucination behavior of generative models.
